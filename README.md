# Smart-Sudoku-Solver-App-Android-

The ultimate Android app designed for Sudoku enthusiasts and puzzle solvers. 
Developed using Java and Android Studio, and leveraging the power of OpenCV and Tesseract for live detection, this app provides real-time solutions to unsolved Sudoku puzzles. 
Simply point your device at an unsolved puzzle, and watch as our sophisticated backtracking algorithm works its magic, offering you instant solutions. Ideal for both beginners and advanced players, our app enhances your Sudoku experience, making it more enjoyable and educational.
